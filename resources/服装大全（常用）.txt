A-line Dress: Fitted at the waist and gradually flares out, resembling the shape of an A. Universally flattering and versatile. [A字裙:腰部合身,逐渐向下展开,呈A形,既通用又多变.]
Sheath Dress: Form-fitting and straight-cut, highlighting the body's silhouette. Often worn for a sleek and sophisticated look. [紧身裙:贴身直裁,突出身体轮廓.通常用于展现流畅而精致的外观.]
Shift Dress: Loose and relaxed, falling straight down from the shoulders. Known for its comfortable and effortless style. [宽松裙:从肩部垂直下落,以其舒适而不费力的风格而闻名.]
Maxi Dress: Floor-length dress that offers elegance and comfort. Suitable for casual and formal occasions. [长裙:及地长裙,提供优雅与舒适.适合休闲和正式场合.]
Wrap Dress: Secures by wrapping one side across the other and tying at the waist, creating a V-neckline and adjustable fit. [裹身裙:通过一侧包裹在另一侧并在腰部系带来固定,形成V领和可调节的合身度.]
Fit and Flare Dress: Fitted at the waist and flares out at the hips, creating a feminine and playful silhouette. [合身裙摆裙:腰部合身,臀部向外展开,营造出女性化且俏皮的轮廓.]
Empire Waist Dress: Features a high waistline just below the bust, with a loose-fitting skirt that flows gracefully. [高腰裙:腰线位于胸部下方,裙摆宽松流畅.]
Off-Shoulder Dress: Highlights the shoulders and neckline by exposing them, offering a romantic and chic appearance. [露肩裙:通过露出肩膀和颈部来突出,呈现出浪漫而时髦的外观.]
Peplum Dress: Embellished with a peplum ruffle at the waist, adding volume and flair to the dress. [荷叶边裙:腰部装饰有荷叶边褶皱,为裙子增添体积和风采.]
Halter Neck Dress: Features straps that wrap around the neck, leaving the shoulders and back exposed. [挂颈裙:特点是围绕颈部的带子,使肩部和背部暴露.]
Bodycon Dress: Body-hugging and figure-flattering, accentuating the wearer's curves.  [紧身裙:紧贴身体,突出穿着者的曲线.]
